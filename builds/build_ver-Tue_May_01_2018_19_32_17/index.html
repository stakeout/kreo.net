KREO: BIM Construction with Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence for better and faster BIM design collaboration and construction cost estimating, scheduling and bidding.

What is Kreo?

Kreo is an advanced BIM design collaboration and BIM construction solution. It harnesses the power of Artificial Intelligence to transform the construction project collaboration, quantity take-off, cost estimating, scheduling and bidding processes.

It brings together all your BIM construction planning resources in a single, cloud-based integrated platform that reduces friction, improves the collaboration and productivity of your team. This gives all your stakeholders a powerful tool to improve the quality of project planning and reduce bidding costs.
